A few quick answers

The things people ask us most. Still stuck? The email above is the fastest way through.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?+
Subscriptions are handled by the App Store, not by us. Open Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your device to change plans or cancel. Cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid the next charge.
The photo scan isn't reading my spelling list+
Good light and a flat, uncrumpled page make the biggest difference. Make sure the words fill most of the frame and aren't cut off at the edges. If it's still struggling, email us the photo and we'll take a look.
Can I use it on more than one device?+
Yes — sign in with the same Apple ID and tap Restore Purchases on the new device to bring your subscription across.
Why does my child's word sound a little different?+
Phonogram Fun uses Australian voices and spelling conventions. You can adjust the voice, speed and strictness anytime in the app's settings to suit your learner.
I found a bug or have an idea+
Brilliant — that's exactly what we want to hear. Email phonogramfun@gmail.com with as much detail as you can, and we'll get on it.
